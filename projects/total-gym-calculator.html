What is it?

When I was using our Total Gym (See picture 4 if you have never seen a Total Gym) I wondered how much weight I was actually lifting. I decided I would make this calculator so my family and I could find the weight at different levels. To calculate this, it just needs two values. First you input your body weight (Red Light), then input your incline level (Blue Light). It will calculate how much weight you are lifting (neglecting friction), then show it on the display (Green Light).

What did I learn?

This is written in C and only uses the AVR Libc library. The main reason I choose to make this physical calculator, and not just an app, was because I wanted to try using this library. In order to make this work, I had to interact with the ATmega328p at a low level. This means I learned how to set bits in the registers manually, and worked with devices like the Analog to Digital converter.
