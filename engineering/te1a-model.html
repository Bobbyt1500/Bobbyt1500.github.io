What is it?

The Mamod TE1A is a model steam traction engine. It works similarly to a steam train as the water in the boiler gets heated up to produce steam. The steam is then used to power the oscillating steam piston. I designed it in Fusion 360 mostly for practice, but also so that I could animate and move different parts.
